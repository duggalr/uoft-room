Welcome to the Uoft Room

Who's going to be in my CSC207 class this coming fall semester? Which students have done an internship at the WWF organization? Any other people on campus listen to the 'Highway Robbery' band? When Facebook first started, these were the types of questions one could easily find answers too. However, zuck (sorta) screwed it all up... This isn't necessarily a replica of the 2004 (the)facebook. And I'm not saying answers to the above questions can't be found leveraging existing social tools (i'm not from the stone age even though the UI of this site might convince you otherwise). I feel in general though, there (shockingly) doesn't really seem to me a medium on the web that allows one to create their online identity and browse through others in a localized community. Every social platform is feels 'super-general' now... that's why I quickly created this as an experiment to see if people would join the party... who knows, I could be completely wrong and this is the most useless piece-of-shit website you have ever seen and you absolutely never want to meet me (given the campus size and current state of the web (ahem), that's very likely anyways) but hey, I'm willing to roll the dice.
