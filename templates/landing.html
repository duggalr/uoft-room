Welcome to 's Room!

The UofT room is an online "room" for current UofT students and alumni. You can create your online profile, add your courses, link your social media accounts, find other students on campus, and share information with others. The website is only available to those who have a valid uoft email address. There's currently 50 students in the room.

It can be used to:

  • Find people who are taking the same classes as you this coming semester
  • Connect with a grad student who's in a program you want to be in
  • Share and interact with other students
